About

Hello, I'm Bradley Turner and I'm a 26 year old packaging designer at abbe with a Honours Degree in Industrial Design. I also run a small business, Algebuckina Design, in my spare time.

I'm looking at getting into a creative design roll that's a little more challenging and diverse than cardboard boxes. I am proficient in SolidWorks and marking up drawing sheets. I'm also a very keen model builder, always wanting to design and build something new for myself or others.

I love a challenge and thrive when I'm thrown into the deep end. Although I have a degree in industrial design, I've worked on coding, virtual reality, web design, content creation, streaming and event projects after I graduating to push myself and learn new things.
